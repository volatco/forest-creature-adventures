Panel 1 – Forest Clearing

Fox is standing proudly. Hedgehog waddles in.

Fox:
Have you heard about Volatco?

Hedgehog:
Is it a new kind of snack?

Panel 2 – Fox gestures toward an imaginary computer

Fox:
Better. It’s a 144-core computer.

Hedgehog:
One hundred and forty-four?!

Panel 3 – Hedgehog counting on his fingers

Hedgehog:
I only have one core.

Fox:
Yes… we’ve noticed.

Panel 4 – Fox explaining enthusiastically

Fox:
Each core runs its own tiny Forth machine.

Hedgehog:
Tiny machines inside a machine?
That sounds like squirrels running a nut warehouse.

Panel 5 – Fox nodding confidently

Fox:
Exactly. Except the squirrels never lose the nuts.

Hedgehog:
Impossible. I once lost a nut while holding it.

Panel 6 – Hedgehog thinking hard

Fox:
That’s why Volatco is superior to other computers.

Hedgehog:
So… it could remember where I buried my snacks?

Fox:
No.

Hedgehog (smiling):
Then it’s already smarter than me.
